PUT YOUR VIDEO FILES HERE
=========================

Supported formats:
- .mp4 (recommended)
- .avi
- .mov
- .wmv
- .webm
- .mkv
- .m4v
- .3gp

After adding videos:
1. Open the calendar
2. Press ESC to enter Editor Mode
3. Go to "Video Schedule" tab
4. Click "Refresh Videos"
5. Your videos will appear in the dropdowns

Tips:
- Keep file names simple (no special characters)
- Test videos by going to: http://localhost:8080/your-video-name.mp4
- Videos play automatically at scheduled times
